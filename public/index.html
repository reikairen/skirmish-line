How to Use Skirmish Line

Objective

Two sides compete over a line of 9 nodes. Secure 5 nodes total or 3 nodes in a row to win. Each side starts with 6 tiles drawn from a shared pool of 54.

On Your Turn

  • 1. Tap a tile in your hand to select it (it lifts up).
  • 2. Tap a glowing node on the board to place it there. Each side of a node holds up to 3 tiles.
  • 3. Press "Done — End Turn" to draw a new tile and pass to your opponent.

How Nodes Are Won

When both sides of a node have 3 tiles, the stronger formation wins that node automatically. Formations ranked strongest to weakest:

#FormationWhat It Means
5Category SequenceSame color, consecutive values (e.g. Red 4-5-6)
4TripletThree tiles with the same number (e.g. 7-7-7)
3Category SetSame color, any values (e.g. Blue 2-5-9)
2SequenceConsecutive values, mixed colors (e.g. 3-4-5)
1TotalHighest total value wins (tiebreaker for equal formations)

Tile Colors

There are 6 colors (categories). Each color has tiles numbered 1 through 9 — one of each, for 54 tiles total. Matching colors is key to building Category Sets and Category Sequences.

Tips

  • Focus on 3 adjacent nodes for a quick win.
  • A Triplet (e.g. 9-9-9 = 27) beats a Category Set even with a lower total.
  • Watch what your opponent places — if they can't beat your formation, that node is yours.

Online Modes
